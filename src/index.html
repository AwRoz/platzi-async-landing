Andrew Roz @Andrew-Roz

In 2016 he won first place at Sibelius guitar festival for his composition and metal interpretation of the traditional English folk song Greensleeves. Continuing forward to 2017 he participated in ‘Ibanez flying fingers’ and landed a spot in the top ten. Having collaborated with bands from across the world like the Italian ‘Ancient knights’ and ‘Tales and Legends’ featuring Göran Edman (former Singer of Yngwie Malmsteen) and German band FB1964 featuring Tim ripper Owens (vocalist for YM) This is a few extracts from the library of Colombian musician and music composer Andrew Roz. Although not coming from a musical background Andrew developed a strong bond and love for music from a very early age, which undoubtedly carried through to his adult life. As he was growing into his teens his passion for music became even stronger and he began his journey to learn the guitar while dreaming of becoming a profesional musician. Self thought through the majority of his life Andrew studied and practiced guitar by himself and in 2008 at the age of 18 he began to compose his own songs. Wanting to share his music and songcovers he started his own YouTube channel Andrew Roz (formerly known as andrewyjm) which today has over 20 000 subscribers. Roz’s music transcends from heavy and powerful to deep and melodic sounds, examples from his own catalog ‘Time traveller’ light and airy classical inspired sounds to beautifully intricate and deeply emotional songs like ‘The other side’ and ‘6000 miles away’ to the more heavy and riff based ‘Beyond the Mirror’. Andrew is constantly making music either in front of the camera or behind the scenes and is always aspiring to make an impact creating meaningful and emotional melodies that people feel a connection to. Currently Andrew is, amongst other projects, working in a Latin American band called ‘Errantes’ founded by himself and his best friend guitarist Juan Quintero where they cover and rearrange ballads from Latin America.
